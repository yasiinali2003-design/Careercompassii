## Design Philosophy

The new Urakompassi logo is a **unique, sophisticated design** that goes beyond a simple compass to embody the core mission: **guiding students to discover their career paths**.

## Key Design Elements

### 1. **Career Pathway Dots** ‚≠ê
- **12 dots surrounding the compass** (alternating amber and green)
- Represents the **8 career personality categories** in the system
- Symbolizes **multiple career possibilities** radiating outward
- Each dot = a potential career direction to explore

### 2. **Dynamic Compass Needle** üß≠
- **North needle (red):** Your primary career direction
- **South needle (teal):** Your foundation and stability
- **East-West needles (green):** Balance and exploration
- Creates a **4-directional cross** = comprehensive guidance in all directions

### 3. **Inner Dashed Ring** üîÑ
- **Career pathway ring** with dashed pattern
- Gradient from green to teal
- Represents the **journey** and **multiple paths** to your destination
- Not a straight line - career paths have curves and choices

### 4. **Center Hub** üéØ
- **Three-layered center:**
  - Outer: Amber (warmth, guidance)
  - Middle: White (clarity, individual)
  - Inner: Teal dot (the student at the center)
- **The individual is at the center** of all career choices

### 5. **Cardinal Direction Markers** üî§
- **U-R-A-K** instead of traditional N-S-E-W
- Spells "URAK" from **Urakompassi**
- Unique brand identifier
- Nordic/Finnish identity

### 6. **Subtle Depth & Shadows** ‚ú®
- Soft drop shadows on needles and housing
- Radial gradient background (light blue)
- Professional, modern aesthetic
- Not flat - has dimensional quality

### 7. **Precision Tick Marks** üìê
- 12 subtle tick marks (every 30¬∞)
- Represents **precision** and **structured approach**

## Usage Guidelines

### ‚úÖ **Do:**
- Use on light backgrounds (white, light gray)
- Maintain minimum size: 40px height
- Keep clear space around logo (20% of height)
- Use SVG format when possible (scalability)

### ‚ùå **Don't:**
- Stretch or distort the logo
- Change colors arbitrarily
- Add effects (shadows, glows) to the SVG
- Place on busy backgrounds without white box
